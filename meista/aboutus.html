Our story


Our café kuppila 11 opened its doors in January 2022 in the same red beach kiosk, which has been in the same place since 1967. The product range has been extended when possible. After daily talking to customers, we found out that there were lot of interest in visiting idyllic log cabin and enjoying coffee also inside.

In winter 2021-2022 the interior of the cafe got its final shape and the patio was completed day before the biggest student event of Turku.

Our cafe is also known for delicious Cappucino, which is made with love from the best ingridients.
