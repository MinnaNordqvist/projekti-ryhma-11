Coffee


Espresso
- Two shots of espresso in one cup.

Americano - American style espresso
- Two shots of espresso, with 2-10 cl of hot water. Served in a cappucino cup.

Espresso Macchiato - marked espresso
- Espresso, with foamed milk on top of it.

Cappuccino
- One shot of espresso, steamed milk and milk foam.

Latte Macchiato
- One shot of espresso poured on top of foamed milk, served in a tall glass. Italian word latte macchiato means stained milk, which refers to the coffee brown stain remaining on top of foam.

Cafe Latte
- Two shots of espresso with steamed milk. Served in a large 0,4l cup.

Cafe Mocca
- One shot of espresso, steamed milk foam, chocolate sauce and whipped cream.

***We have also other milk options, soy and oat milk and you can add different flavored syrups.
